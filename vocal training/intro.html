what is Vocal training

Considering 60% of what we do in singing is physical, vocal training involves developing physical coordination .

What to expect

You might be wondering, what do I expect during the time you undergo vocal training? The road to becoming a great singer is a lifetime journey,as the voice is a very complex instrument ,we find out that as we progress in the journey,we discover more and more about out voices ,more techniques,greater flexibily and wider range of skill are unlocked.More road are covered,mountains are ,oceans are crossed

Vocal Freedom- The very first thing one should expect is the freedom that emanates in the vocal folds from about a week - 3 weeks depending the voice. The more we move on in this journey ,we get more and more vocal freedom,the tightness we first feel in our throat starts to lessen

Vocal Technique- ALready stated that our voice is a complex tool,the more we progress in our journey,we learn new technique,technique needed to sing.
