Caring

To maximise the efficiency of the guitar,to make it last longer,to help it retain it qualities like tone,beauty and overall characteristics,its important you know how to care for the instrument. So many guitarist lack this important and essential skill and end up having an old and poor quality instrument after couple of months, knowlegde of caring for the guitar is important and there are a lot of ways to care for the guitar.The list would go on and on but the basics of caring for your guitar are;

  1. Clean and polish the entire body of the guitar once or tice a month using a guitar polish and soft cloth
  2. Ensure you clean the fretboard everytime you change the strings or polish the guitar. it should be down once or twice a month
  3. Wipe down the neck and around the body of the guitar with a cloth after playing,it removes the grim,dirt and sweat that accumulate during playing.it also prevents the strings from rusting faster
  4. Never expose the instrument to excessive heat or cold.
  5. Avoid actions that give the guitar a scar( felling on the floor,scratching the body against a rough surface,discarding it at the wrong place after playing etc
  6. Dont tweak the guitar default set up .if you are not sure of what to do,take it to someone who knows better
  7. Change worn out part of the guitar immediately
  8. Change rusty/broken strings immediately or every 4-5 months
  9. At least once in a year,take it to a technician for checkup.
