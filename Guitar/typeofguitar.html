Types Of Guitar

Guitars even with its similar construction ,tuning and other characteristics also differs from one another

A) the acoustic guitar

this class of guitar produce their sound acoustically ,hence the name acoustic guitar.what do we mean by the term "acoustically". when the the strings of an acoustic guitar are plucked or strummed ,vibrations are created.

the vibration created by the motion of the strummed or plucked string are transmitted from the guitar body where it resonates to create a sound wave which is then transmitted through the air which is a medium of transportation for sound waves. This sound wave is then transmitted through the air to our ear where its is perceived as sounds

types of acoustic guitar

they can be classified based on the materials used to make the strings. the string type can be gut/nylon or steel.

GUT/NYLON STRING GUITAR - Classical guitar,vihuela,Baroque guitar,etc

STEEL STRING GUITAR - steel string guitar{AKA folk guitar,western or country guitar}, twelve string guitar, lap steel guitar,arch top guitar

the most commona acoustic guitar used today are the classical guitar,steel string guitar and the lap steel guitar.

B) the electric guitar

unlike the acoustic guitar,which resonates using it body{via the sound hole or box}. the electric guitar doesnt have a sound box or hole.instead it uses a device called PICKUP . it converts the vibration of the strings into electrical signal

Now the signal generated by the electric guitar pickup is too weak to drive a loud speaker . it is therefore plugged into a guitar amplifier before being sent to a loudspeaker whick makes the sound loud and audible enough to hear
