  • guitar

Going deeper and skimming at the surface at the sound the guitar produces,we discover that the sound the guitar make is not just melodic but its also harmonic in nature,Even at breaking down the sound of the guitar into melody and harmony, we discover that we have just scratched the surface and there are many more underlying components working together to form melody and harmony

Diving deeper,we discover that the basic building block of any melody or harmony are major building blocks and the minor building blocks

Major building blocks

  1. scales
  2. chords
  3. key

Minor building blocks

  1. chord progressions
  2. Appergios
  3. modes

Every tone the guitar produces,whether melodic or harmonic in nature are formed from the combinations of these building blocks
