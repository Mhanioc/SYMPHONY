The Lead Guitarist

This guitarist typically play the lead guitar,which basically involves playing melodic lines,instrumental fill/lick passage,solo and occassionaly they play riff in some songs context

ROLES OF THE LEAD GUITARIST IN A BAND

They play the melodic line

When therie are no other lead musicians in the band,the lead guitarist is expected to take the role of playing the melodic line of the song

Playing the lick and instrumental fill passage

Occasionally,if there is another musician like a saxophonist or vocalist already assuming the position of a lead ,the lead guitar would then assume the role of playing licks and fills for the breaks that would occur in the songs

Playing riff

In some genre and song context,especially on where there is another lead instrument,the lead guitarist can also play the riff which most time is usually melodic and the use of chord is limited

Playing Solo

This is one of the most common role of a lead guitarist,in scenario where the lead guitarist play a solo.He/She is usually given a space in the context of a song with another lead instrument to solo or if there is no other lead instrument,he solos through the own song
