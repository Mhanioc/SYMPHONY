The Rhythm Guitarist

This guitarist role is majorly to play rhythm guitar,they provide the rhythmic and harmonic part of the song

Roles

1)They provide the rythmic and harmonic part of a song

In a setting of a song with two guitarist,common in genres like rock,metal and pop.we often discover one of the guitarist is found supplying the rhythmic flow{include the beat and the time} of the song by strumming and also harmonic content which include chord,riffs,etc

2)They back up the lead

The rhythm guitarist always back up the lead,not the lead in a song setting may be the vocalist,lead guitar,flute,violin,saxophone,etc.They always create a supporting line in which the lead musician would be able to solo on
