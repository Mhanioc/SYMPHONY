what is a guitar?

The guitar is one of the most common musical instrument around the world.You probably would have heard of it since childhood.The guitar is primarily classified as a fretted string instrment,it usually have 4 to 18 string but its most common for it to have 6,7 or 12 strings

it is typically played by strumming or picking the strings with the fingers fingernails or even with a pick or plectrum using one hand while fretting (pressing or holding down on the strings against the fretboard) with the fingers of the other hands ,It is common for people to strum with the left hand and fret the guitar with the right hand ,thats why most guiar are left handed guitar

The guitar is a type of chordophone,traditionally,it has its body constructed from wood,plastic and metal,it has its strings constructed from gut,nylon or steel strings.it differs from other chordophone by its construction

Guitar Anatomy And Design

Although,Guitars come in a variety of shapes,sizes,and types,they all have certain characteristics in common. They all have a neck,a body and a tuning head(also known as the headstock or the head).

The Neck

The neck of a guitar is usually fixed to its body with glue or bolt.It is faced with a flat piece of wood usually made with mahogany or ebony wood.Its often has a metal rod running through it to strenghten it.

The neck of the guitar has its finger board divided into sections called fret,this sections are indicated by wires set into the wood called fret wire,Placing your finger on the space between two fret wire(in the middle of a fret)- determine the different pitches and notes that can be formed on each string.
